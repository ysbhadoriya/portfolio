Ram Mandir Photos in Ayodhya: Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Teerth Kshetra releases first look ahead of bhumi pujan

ZeeBiz WebTeam | Aug 05, 2020, 01:07 PM IST
View in App

Ram Mandir Photos in Ayodhya: Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Teerth Kshetra - a trust set up for the construction and management of Shri Ram Temple in Ayodhya - has revealed the first look of the grand Ram Temple model. The trust has been set up in Uttar Pradesh by the Government of India on the directions of the Supreme Court of Indian and in accordance with the title suit judgement. The trust has 15 trustees. Pic Credit: Twitter

1/5

Ram Mandir Photos

The Ram Mandir photos give an insight into how this temple will look from various angles - from inside and outside of the temple. The Ram Temple in Ayodhya will be grander and bigger in comparison to the earlier plan with three new domes now added to the design. The height of the structure has been increased by 20 feet. The new design, which has been approved by the Trust will not only add to the magnificence of the temple, it will also enable more devotees to get accommodated in the precincts of the temple. Pic Credit: Twitter

2/5

Ram Mandir Photos
The Ram Mandir pictures were released on Tuesday by the Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Teerth Kshetra. The pictures of the proposed model of the Ram Temple are testimony to its colossal structure. The temple will be built in Ayodhya - a birthplace of Lord Ram. Pic Credit: Twitter

3/5

Ram Mandir Photos
The Trust took to micro-blogging site Twitter to announce that Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir will be a unique example of Indian architecture. The Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Teerth Kshetra tweeted, "Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Mandir will be a unique example of Indian architecture. Here are some photos of the proposed model. Jai Shri Ram!," it said

The official Twitter Handle @ShriRamTeerth also said that the Ram Temple will be a manifestation of divinity and grandeur. It has been sharing pictures not only of the proposed structure but also of the rituals taking place today.

"Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Mandir will be the manifestation of divinity and grandeur. Some more pictures of the proposed structure," said Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Teerth Kshetra. Pic Credit: Twitter

4/5

Ram Mandir Bhumi Pujan
The puja ceremony has already begun in Ram Mandir, Ayodhya with the offering of special prayers to Lord Hanuman at Hanumangarhi as a part of an old tradition of 'Nishan Puja'. Prime Minister Narendra Modi is now present at the site. Pic Credit: Twitter 

5/5

Ram Mandir Photos
In the wake of this great ceremony, the borders of Ayodhya were sealed on Tuesday and Special Protection Group (SPG) has since been taking care of the entire security system of the holy city. Pic Credit: Twitter
